    Grocery List

    Describe your project vision. Explain the problem you’re trying to solve, and why you believe a database solution is necessary.

    Reason why it's important to us: Sometimes when you get to a store- you realize that they don't have this one thing you're looking for
    Why DB needed: Food products will have many different attributes for cataloguing purposes, and grocery list items need to be consistent especially since it needs to check against the store's own database of products being sold
